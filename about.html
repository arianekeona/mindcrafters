CalmConnect

About Us

We are Mindcrafters, a group of UCI Students in IN4MATX 172: Project in Health Informatics. As a team we are working on the development of CalmConnect - a mobile app intended to aid adolescents and young adults in rural areas take care of mental health from the comfort of their own home.

Our Mission

CalmConnect is a revolutionary mental health app developed to combat the pressing issue of mental health crises among young adults. With a rise in mental health issues, exacerbated by the COVID-19 pandemic, our team recognized the urgent need for accessible and effective solutions. Our app is designed to bridge the gap between individuals and mental health professionals, providing support, resources, and daily challenges based on self-care.
