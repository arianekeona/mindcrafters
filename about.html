About

We are Mindcrafters, a group of UCI Students in IN4MATX 172: Project in Health Informatics. As a team we are working on the development of CalmConnect - a mobile app intended to assist adolescants and young adults in rural areas take care of mental health from the comfort of their own home.

Our mission with CalmConnect is to provide a solution to the mental health crisis that is affecting teenagers and young adults across the US. With CalmConnect we aim to supply assistance to our young users in caring for their wellbeing and equipping them with the necessary resources and engaging activities to maintain their mental health.
