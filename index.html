Welcome to CalmQuest

CalmQuest is your mental health companion that helps in your navigation of the complexities of mental health. Our app offers a welcoming environment for young adults to explore, engage, and find solace in their mental health journey. Whether you're seeking support, resources, or simply a listening ear, CalmQuest is here to accompany you every step of the way.

The CalmQuest Experience

Personalized, calm quests, and earning badges.


Specify your mental health symptoms and needs to receive tailored resources.

Complete daily "quests" that are help care for your social, physical, and mental health.

Earn and unlock badges for reaching milestones in quest completion.

The Design Process

Take a look at our team's design process in creating CalmQuest.

Low-Fidelity Sketches

Once we decided on our main features for the app, our team member, Ariane, sketched out some low-fidelity wireframes of the app solution. Depicted here are sketches for the home page, quest page, and profile page. The sketches were based on our group's finalized set of features: calm quests, badges, and a matching feature.

Mid-Fidelity Wireframes

From then on, Ariane transferred the low-fidelity sketches into mid-fidelity wireframes on Figma. The mid-fidelity wireframes that were designed were for the primary pages of the application: the home page with both a weekly and daily view, a specific quest page, a page to complete a quiz that matches the user to mental health resources, and the profile page. We conducted some usability testing on these wireframes to gain insight on how best to implement those ideas into our high-fidelity designs.

High-Fidelity Mockups

After consolidating our ideas from the mid and lo-fi wireframes, our group moved on to designing the high-fidelity mockups of CalmConnect. We first decided on a typeface and color palette that was both engaging and relaxing to the eyes. With regards to the mockups themselves, they consist of our finalized features: offering daily calm quests with progress trackers and guided tips, earning badges for completing the calm quests, and supplying mental health resources based on quiz responses from users.
