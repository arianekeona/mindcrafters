Calm Connect

CalmConnect is a mobile app that provides its users with tailored quests to better their mental health and guides them towards specific resources that provide treatment to any mental health struggles.

Get matched with curated resources.

Take a quiz to inform CalmConnect of your mental health needs, symptoms, and preferences. From then on, receive personalized resources that are within your selected demographics.

Engage in daily "calm quests" to boost your wellbeing.

Through carefully selected activites take some time to connect with your mind, body, and others. CalmConnect provides users with small and reasonable quests each day to encourage users to care for themselves in anyway they can.

Customize your quest experience to be tailored to your interests and needs.

Be able to decide the types of quests that are offered for you and the rate at which you receive them. Specify your circumstances and interests to let CalmConnect know what quests are best fit for you.

Earn badges for completing quests and build up your collection!

Complete your daily calm quests and earn badges each week for hitting milestones in quest completion. Collect a variety of badges and fill up your collection!
