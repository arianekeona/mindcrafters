Offline Mental Health Care

Welcome to CalmConnect, your companion in navigating the complexities of mental health. Our app offers a welcoming environment for young adults to explore, engage, and find solace in their mental health journey. Whether you're seeking support, resources, or simply a listening ear, CalmConnect is here to accompany you every step of the way.

The CalmConnect Experience

Personalized, calm quests, and earning badges.


Specify your mental health symptoms and needs and personal interests.

Complete daily "quests" that are curated to care for your wellbeing.

Earn and unlock badges for reaching milestones in quest completion.

Design Process

Take a look at our team's design process in creating CalmConnect and see how we reached our end product.

Low-Fidelity Wireframes

Once we finalized our main features for the app, our team member, Ariane, sketched out some low-fidelity wireframes of the app solution. Depicted here are sketches for the home page, quest page, and profile page.

Mid-Fidelity Wireframes

From then on, Ariane transferred the low-fidelity wireframes into mid-fidelity wireframes on Figma. This allowed for more detail to be displayed and provided a firm starting point in starting the high-fidelity mockups.

High-Fidelity Mockups

The group worked on Figma to design the high-fidelity mockups from the basis of our mid and low-fidelity wireframes.
