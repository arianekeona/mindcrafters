About Us

We are Mindcrafters, a group of UCI Students in IN4MATX 172: Project in Health Informatics. As a team we are working on the development of CalmConnect - a mobile app intended to aid adolescents and young adults in rural areas take care of mental health from the comfort of their own home.

Our Mission

With CalmConnect we aim to provide a solution to the mental health crisis that is affecting teenagers and young adults across the United States. Our solution is designed to offer assistance to our young audience in caring for their wellbeing and equipping them with the necessary resources to maintain their mental health.
